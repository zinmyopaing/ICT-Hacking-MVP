# Problem Notes

## Selected Case
Smart Study Group Finder

## Target Respondents

| Respondent Type | Why This Respondent Matters | Planned Number | Actual Number |
|----------------|----------------------------|----------------|---------------|
| University Students | They experience the problem of finding study partners and study groups. | 10 | 10 |

## Observed Problem

Students struggle to find study partners for subjects.

## Current Alternatives

| Workaround | Who Uses It? | Weakness / Pain | Evidence Source |
|------------|-------------|-----------------|----------------|
| Facebook Groups | Students | Responses are slow and posts get ignored | Interview |
| Messenger Groups | Students | Difficult to find active members | Interview |
| Class Chat | Students | Messages get buried quickly | Interview |

## Repeated Pain Points

| Pain Point | Number of Mentions | Example Evidence | Severity |
|------------|-------------------|------------------|----------|
| Cannot find active study partners | 8/10 | "No one replied to my post." | High |
| Responses are slow | 6/10 | "It took days to get a response." | Medium |
| Difficult to find same-subject students | 7/10 | "I could not find people studying Java." | High |

## Consequences

| Consequence | Example | Evidence Strength |
|-------------|---------|-------------------|
| Students study alone | Respondents reported studying by themselves | Moderate |
| Learning progress becomes slower | Students take longer to understand difficult topics | Moderate |
| Exam preparation becomes harder | Students cannot share notes or discuss topics | Strong |

## Notes for MVP Direction

Need subject-based search functionality.
Need study group creation and joining features.
Need simple group information display.
Need easy communication between group members.
