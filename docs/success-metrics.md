

# Success Metrics and Decision Rules

## 1. Success Metrics

| Metric ID | Metric | Target | How to Measure | Related Requirement |
|-----------|--------|--------|----------------|---------------------|
| M-01 | Homepage understanding rate | 90% or higher | Users correctly explain the purpose of the website after viewing the Homepage. | FR-01 |
| M-02 | Study group search success rate | 90% or higher | Users successfully find a suitable study group using the Find Groups page. | FR-04 |
| M-03 | Join Group completion rate | 80% or higher | Users successfully complete and submit the Join Group form without assistance. | FR-07 |
| M-04 | Create Group completion rate | 80% or higher | Users successfully create a new study group by completing the Create Group form. | FR-06 |
| M-05 | Overall usability rating | Average 4.0 out of 5 or higher | Average rating collected from the feedback form after testing. | FR-01 to FR-08 |

---

## 2. Decision Rules

| Result | Decision |
|--------|----------|
| All success metrics meet or exceed the target values. | Continue to the next development phase and implement the final prototype. |
| One or two success metrics do not meet the target values. | Improve the affected pages, simplify the user interface, and conduct another round of usability testing. |
| More than two success metrics fail to meet the target values. | Review the project requirements, user stories, navigation flow, and prototype design before continuing development. |
