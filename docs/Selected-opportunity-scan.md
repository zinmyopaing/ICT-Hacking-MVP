# Selected Semester Project Opportunity

## Selected Project Title
Smart Study Group Finder

## Target User
University students, tutors, and academic support staff.

## Problem Statement
Students often struggle to find classmates or peers who are studying the same subjects and are interested in forming study groups. As a result, many students study alone and miss opportunities for collaborative learning and knowledge sharing.

## Current Alternative
Students usually search for study partners through class group chats, social media groups, or personal networks.

## Proposed IT Solution
A web-based study group finder platform where students can create study groups, join existing groups, search for groups by subject or course, and communicate with other members.

## Why This Opportunity Was Selected
It received a high NUF score because it solves a real student problem and is feasible to develop as a web-based MVP.

## Why This Is Feasible Without IoT or Advanced Cybersecurity
The project only requires a website interface, user authentication, database storage, and search functionality.

## Possible MVP Type
Web App Prototype

## Initial Validation Plan for Lab 03
Interview university students and collect feedback about difficulties in finding study groups and study partners.
