Lab 05 Practice Case Brief: Smart Study Group Finder

Case Context

University students often struggle to find suitable study groups, study partners, or tutors for their courses, assignments, and exam preparation. They usually ask friends, post messages in class chat groups, search through social media, or rely on word of mouth. These current workarounds are inconsistent, time-consuming, difficult to organize, and not well managed.

Target Users

* University students looking for study groups or study partners
* Tutors who want to provide academic support
* Academic support staff who organize and monitor study activities

Product Direction

The proposed product is a web-based Smart Study Group Finder platform that allows users to search study groups by subject, create and join study groups, match with students based on schedules and interests, discover tutors, receive study session notifications, and manage study group activities through one centralized system.

Lab 05 Practice Purpose

This practice case is only for learning the method. After practicing with this case, each group must apply the same method to the group’s own selected case from Lab 02 and Lab 03.








Lab 06 Practice Case Brief

Case: Smart Study Group Finder

University students often struggle to find suitable study groups, study partners, or tutors for their courses, assignments, and exam preparation. Current alternatives include asking friends, posting in class chat groups, searching through social media, contacting tutors individually, or studying alone. These methods are often time-consuming, unorganized, and ineffective.

Target Users

* University students looking for study groups or study partners
* Tutors who organize and provide academic support
* Academic support staff who manage and promote learning activities

Product Direction

A web-based Smart Study Group Finder system that allows users to search for study groups by subject, create and join study groups, discover tutors, view study group details, manage schedules, receive notifications about upcoming study sessions, and provide an admin panel for managing users, study groups, and tutoring activities.

Lab 06 Focus

Use this case only for practice. Students must apply the same method to their own group case selected in Lab 02 and investigated in Lab 03. The focus of Lab 06 is to transform the project requirements into a functional prototype, validate the user flow, improve usability, and ensure that all core MVP features are connected to the final system design.
