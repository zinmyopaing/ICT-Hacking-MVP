1. Project Title

Smart Study Group Finder

⸻

2. Target User

Primary user: University students who want to find or create study groups for their courses.

Secondary user: Tutors or academic support staff who help students organize study groups.

⸻

3. Problem Statement

Students often struggle to find classmates who are studying the same subject. Most students rely on class group chats or social media, making it difficult to find suitable study partners and organize effective study groups.

⸻

4. Proposed IT Product

A web-based study group finder that allows students to create study groups, search available groups, view group details, send join requests, and manage their joined groups through an easy-to-use platform.

⸻

5. Core Value Proposition

The system helps students quickly find study partners, join suitable study groups, and collaborate more effectively, reducing the time and effort needed to organize group study.

⸻

6. MVP Scope

The MVP will focus on:

* User registration and login
* Search study groups
* View group details
* Create study groups
* Join study groups
* View request status
* Manage My Groups
* View and edit user profile

⸻

7. Out of Scope for Current Semester

* AI-based group recommendation
* Real-time chat or video meeting
* Payment or subscription system
* Mobile application
* Advanced analytics and reporting
* University system integration
